Ariana Grande's Lyric Video

In Ariana Grande's “One More Time”, the narrator (Ariana) plays the role of someone in a broken romance,
and admittedly it is ‘her fault’. In other words, she lied to her ex. How exactly she deceived him is never specified.
However, the implication is that she cheated on him. And now she is reeling as a result of their breakup since he holds a very-special place in her heart.
